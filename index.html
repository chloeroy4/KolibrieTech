We believe technology should be ethical, sustainable, and open.

Here is how we work towards our goals to make your experience better for you, better for the planet.

Fair Labor Practices

Ensure that all workers involved in the design, production, and distribution of your products are paid fairly and work in safe, ethical conditions.

Transparent Supply Chain

Ensure all suppliers comply with fair labor standards and increase transparency around working conditions throughout the supply chain.

CHECK OUR SHIPMENT AND SUPPLY CHAIN

Recycled & Sustainable Materials

Use recycled metals and plastics for at least 70% of casings and components by 2027, and keep improving circularity.

Sustainable Shipping Commitment

Ship 100% of our logistics by sea or low-emission methods by 2026 to reduce CO₂ footprint.

Certify for Circular Economy

By 2028, earn Cradle to Cradle, EPEAT Gold, or TCO Certification for every upgrade component we produce.
