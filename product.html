Why do we care?

At Kolibrietech we work towards our goals to create laptops and desk computers that do not become obsolete after just a few years, that are strong, powerful and durable.

Design for longevity:

Promote the idea that powerful, high-quality technology can also be sustainable and repairable — not disposable.

Redefine innovation:

Shift consumer mindset from “newest is best” to “most maintainable, repairable, and long-lasting is best.”

Fair Labor Practices:

Ensure that all workers involved in the design, production, and distribution of your products are paid fairly and work in safe, ethical conditions.

Transparent supply chain:

Ensure all suppliers comply with fair labor standards and increase transparency around working conditions throughout the supply chain.

Use recycled and Sustainable materials:

Commit to using recycled metals and plastics for at least 70% of all laptop casings and non-electronic components by 2027, and continuously improve material circularity.

Achieve Carbon Neutral Offices:

Power all offices and facilities with 100% clean, renewable electricity by 2026 and implement carbon offsetting to become a carbon-neutral organization by 2028.

Sustainable Shipping Commitment:

Ship 100% of our global logistics by sea or low-emission methods by 2026, significantly cutting down our transportation-related CO₂ footprint.

Global Reforestation and Climate Action:

We partner with reforestation initiatives and community-led climate projects to plant one tree per device sold.

Make Repairability a Right, Not a Feature:

We support the “Right to Repair” legislation globally, and offer public repair guides, open-source schematics, we sell all the laptop components directly, make the upgrade compatible, and repairability scores for every product we release.

Close the Digital Divide:

We offer educational discounts, and donation programs to improve digital access for students, NGOs, and low-income communities worldwide.

Certify Products for Circular Economy:

By 2028, achieve third-party certifications (Cradle to Cradle, EPEAT Gold, or TCO Certified) for every laptop upgrade component we produce.

Create a Repair Skill Academy:

To improve our online repair literacy platform, with free videos and tutorials- training in electronics repair to empower consumers.
