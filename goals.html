Why do we care?

OUR GOALS

This is what we care about — turning our mission into a lasting vision through these 12 goals.

1. Design for longevity Promote the idea that powerful, high-quality technology can also be sustainable and repairable — not disposable.
2. Redefine innovation Shift consumer mindset from “newest is best” to “most maintainable, repairable, and long-lasting is best.”
3. Fair Labor Practices Ensure all workers in the design, production, and distribution of our products are paid fairly and work in safe, ethical conditions.
4. Transparent supply chain Ensure all suppliers comply with fair labor standards and increase transparency around working conditions.
5. Use recycled and sustainable materials Commit to using recycled metals and plastics for at least 70% of laptop casings and non-electronic parts by 2027.
6. Achieve Carbon Neutral Offices Power all facilities with 100% renewable electricity by 2026 and offset carbon to become carbon neutral by 2028.
7. Sustainable Shipping Commitment Ship 100% of global logistics by sea or low-emission methods by 2026 to cut transportation CO₂ emissions.
8. Global Reforestation and Climate Action We plant one tree per device sold by partnering with reforestation and community-led climate projects.
9. Make Repairability a Right Support Right to Repair laws, publish open-source guides, sell all components, and provide repairability scores.
10. Close the Digital Divide Offer discounts and donation programs to increase digital access for students, NGOs, and low-income groups.
11. Certify Products for Circular Economy By 2028, achieve certifications like Cradle to Cradle, EPEAT Gold, or TCO Certified for all upgrade components.
12. Create a Repair Skill Academy Launch an online platform with free tutorials and training in electronics repair to empower consumers.
